## Case01
    Synchronous Buck Converter
### 描述
    . Topology:         Synchronous Buck Converter
    . Input voltage：   30Vdc
    . Output voltage:   15Vdc
    . Switch frequency: 50kHz
    . Rated power:      75W
    . Verison:          Maltab 2016b
    . Control method:   Single voltage close loop with a single PI controller
